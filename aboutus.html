About Us

Who Are We

We are a group of engineering students at San Jose State University who have worked to create this app to help the rest of the student community at SJSU purchase groceries and daily essentials for less.

Our Mission Statement

The SJSU Sparket app technilogies enable students purchase groceries from the comfort of their dorms. It allows students to purchase groceries and daily essentials for cheaper prices from supermarkets outside of campus.
